Game of Thrones

Mega-fan page

Here is a collection of some of my favorite scenes from Game of Thrones:

Season 1 Episode 1: Winter is Coming. The Starks pay homage to King Robert as he arrives at Winterfell.

Season 1 Episode 10: Fire and Blood. Daenerys Targaryen walks out of a pyre, her newborn dragons clinging to her.

Season 2 Episode 18: The Prince of Winterfell. Ygritte tells Jon Snow how much he knows. (Spoiler: it's nothing.)

Season 2 Episode 20: Battle of the Blackwater. Tyrion defends King's Landing, setting the bay ablaze with Wildfire.

Season 3 Episode 26: The Climb. Jon Snow and the wildlings face a daunting climb over the wall.

Season 3 Episode 29: The Rain of Castamere. Betrayed by Walder Frey, Robb Stark awaits his fate at the Red Wedding.

Season 4 Episode 34: Oathkeeper. Jaime Lannister presents Brienne Tarth with his Valyrian steel sword, Oathkeeper.

Season 4 Episode 38: The Mountain and the Viper. Champions fight for Tyrion and Cersei at thier trial.

Season 4 Episode 49: The Dance of Dragons. Dany approaches Drogon in the pits.

Season 5 Episode 50: Mother's Mercy. Jon Snow lies in a pool of blood after being betrayed by his brothers.

Season 6 Episode 53: Oathbreaker. Bran learns the truth about his 'brother' Jon Snow.

Season 6 Episode 59: Battle of the Bastards. Bolton and Snow meet on the battlefield.
